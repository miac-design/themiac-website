About

AI that amplifies human potential

I believe technology should lift people up, not leave them behind.

As a researcher, strategist, and community builder, I work at the intersection of artificial intelligence, accessibility, and human impact. My academic research explored how technology can make experiences accessible to those traditionally left out — and that mission continues in everything I do.

Today, I lead marketing at Austin AI Hub, conduct AI research at Rackspace, and advise organizations on implementing AI responsibly. I don't just theorize — I build, I teach, I ship.

My Mission

To advance AI systems that protect vulnerable populations and make technology accessible to underserved communities — proving that ethical AI isn't just possible, it's essential.
